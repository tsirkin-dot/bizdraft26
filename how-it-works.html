How the AI drafts

Tailored to your situation. Honest about what it is.

BizDraft uses AI as a finished product — not as a chat prompt you have to engineer. Here's what that means in practice.

Tailored, not templated

Every clause is written around what you described — the work, the parties, the payment. Not a static template with your name pasted in.

State-aware

You stay in control

Honest answers

What people ask before they trust it.

Why not just use ChatGPT?

You can. The difference is the work in between. BizDraft already knows what a service agreement should contain, which clauses your state requires, and how to ask the right questions to fill them in. ChatGPT is a blank prompt — you have to know what to ask for. BizDraft is AI as a finished product.

Is an AI-drafted agreement legally binding?

A signed agreement is binding under the same rules as any other — both parties competent, clear terms, lawful purpose, signatures. The drafting tool doesn't change that.

What we won't claim is that any agreement is right for any deal. For high-stakes engagements, hand the Word file to your attorney before you sign. The agreement is yours to take wherever it needs to go.

How long does it actually take?

The intake takes a minute or two. Generation takes about 90 seconds. Inline edits depend on how much you customize. Most users finish in under five minutes.

Does BizDraft store my data?

The agreement you draft lives in your browser tab, not on our server. Close the tab and it's gone on our side. We'll prompt you to download before you go. See Trust & privacy for the full story.
