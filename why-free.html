The business model

How BizDraft can be free.

BizDraft is built and funded by airSlate — the company behind SignNow, pdfFiller, and DocHub. airSlate's paid products handle e-signature, PDF editing, and document workflows for over 100 million users worldwide.

BizDraft solves one specific thing — the drafting — for free. The cost of drafting is paid by airSlate, on the bet that some people who use BizDraft will go on to sign, send, or refine their document. If they do, they'll meet airSlate's paid products on the way out.

If they don't, that's fine too. The draft is yours either way.

What this means for you

  • The drafting itself stays free, with no quota and no "premium tier."
  • When you're done, you'll see suggestions for SignNow, pdfFiller, and DocHub. They're sister products, all free to try, all optional.
  • You can use any e-signature tool you already have. Word and PDF downloads work everywhere.
  • We're not selling your data. There's no data to sell — your draft never leaves your browser tab.

What we won't do

  • Add a paywall to the drafting flow.
  • Require an account to download.
  • Sell or share the contents of your draft.
  • Lock your file in a proprietary format.

What happens after the draft.

BizDraft handles the drafting. When you're ready to sign, send, or refine the document, these sister tools from airSlate take it the rest of the way — all free to try.

SignNow is the eSignature solution built for your entire organization — giving the whole team everything they need to send and sign documents, without user limits or complex setup.

pdfFiller is a complete document solution for individuals and SMBs, making it easy to edit, sign, and share documents in one place.

DocHub is a document management solution that combines advanced PDF editing, eSignatures, forms, and team collaboration with seamless Google Workspace integration.

Honest answers

The questions that come up first.

Is BizDraft really free?

Yes. No paywall, no trial, no credit card. There's no account because there's nothing on our side to sign into.

What's the catch?

The catch is the SignNow / pdfFiller / DocHub recommendations at the end. Those are airSlate's paid products. If you want an e-signature tool, our suggestions are right there. If you don't, you can ignore them — your Word file works anywhere.

Will the free tier turn into a paid one later?

The drafting itself stays free. That's the whole product. If we ever add anything beyond the drafting (a team workspace, batch generation, that sort of thing), those would be separate offerings.

Are there download limits?

For most use, no. We do soft-cap downloads per browser session to keep automation off the service. You won't see it during normal use.

Will my data be sold?

No. The draft you make lives in your browser tab. We don't keep it after you close the tab, and there's nothing to sell. See Trust & privacy for the full story.

Why is BizDraft only for U.S. small businesses?

State law matters for these agreements, and we wanted to get the state-aware drafting right before going broader. V1 is U.S., all 50 states. Other jurisdictions are on the roadmap.
