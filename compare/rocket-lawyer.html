If you need one contract for one project, paying $39.99/month doesn't make sense. BizDraft generates the draft for free with no ongoing commitment.

Template-fill tools produce standard clauses regardless of input nuance. BizDraft's AI reads your description and adjusts the language accordingly — payment structure, scope limits, IP handling.

Rocket Lawyer wins

If your business creates dozens of agreements per month and you want a library of templates with attorney oversight, Rocket Lawyer's subscription model may deliver more value per dollar.

Honest verdict

Which one should you actually use?

If your need is simple — one agreement, now, free, no friction — BizDraft is the faster and cheaper path. You don't create an account, you don't pay anything, and you get a tailored draft that goes beyond fill-in-the-blanks.
