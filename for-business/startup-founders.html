The founder risk map

Five risks that kill early startups. All preventable.

Co-founder split

One founder leaves, taking equity and moral authority. Fix: vesting schedule + buyout clause in the co-founder agreement.

IP chain gap

Pre-formation work belongs to the person, not the company. Fix: IP assignment signed before or immediately after incorporation.

Leaked pitch deck

Investor or partner uses your roadmap to build a competing product. Fix: mutual NDA before every material conversation.

Contractor owns the product

Developer you hired keeps IP because you never got it in writing. Fix: IC agreement with IP assignment on every contractor.

Misclassification exposure

Early "contractors" treated like employees. Fix: IC agreement with proper scope, rate, and independence language.
