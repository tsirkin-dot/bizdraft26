The solo founder risk map

Five risks. All fixable before you start.

Scope creep

Every "can you just add one more thing" chips away at margin. Fix: SOW with change-order clause and revision limits.

Non-payment

Client uses the work and ghosts the invoice. Fix: deposit upfront, IP transfer on full payment, late-fee clause.

Subcontractor IP

Helper you brought in claims ownership of what they built. Fix: IC agreement with IP assignment before any work begins.

Client poaches your sub

Client hires your contractor directly, cutting you out. Fix: non-solicitation clause in the service agreement.

No exit clause

Project goes sideways and you have no clean way out. Fix: termination clause with kill-fee and notice period.
