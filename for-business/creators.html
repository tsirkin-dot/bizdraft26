The creator risk map

Five creator-specific risks. All preventable.

Unlimited content usage

Brand runs your content as ads for years after the campaign. Fix: content licensing agreement with defined channels, duration, and paid-use fees.

FTC exposure

Unclear disclosure obligation in the campaign brief. Fix: FTC-aware disclosure clause confirming both sides' obligations.

Kill-fee gap

Brand cancels after you've produced the content. Fix: kill-fee clause tied to production stage completion.

Exclusivity overreach

Brand claims broad exclusivity across an entire category. Fix: exclusivity clause with specific brands listed, not whole categories.

Production IP

Editor or videographer claims ownership of produced content. Fix: IC agreement with IP assignment before production starts.
