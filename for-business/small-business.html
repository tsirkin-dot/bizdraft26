The SMB risk map

Five risks small businesses face most. All preventable.

No client contract

Client disputes the scope or refuses to pay. Fix: service agreement with payment milestones and suspension rights.

Contractor misclassification

IRS audit reclassifies contractors as employees. Fix: IC agreement with proper independence and scope language.

IP ownership gap

Contractor or employee claims ownership of work product. Fix: IP assignment clause in every employment and IC agreement.

Vendor failure

Supplier doesn't deliver; you lose the client. Fix: vendor agreement with delivery obligations and remedy clause.

Confidentiality leak

Ex-employee takes customer list to a competitor. Fix: NDA with confidentiality and non-solicitation clauses for all hires.
