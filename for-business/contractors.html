The contractor risk map

Five job-site risks. All preventable in the contract.

Scope dispute

Client says "we agreed that included X." Fix: detailed scope section plus change-order clause requiring written sign-off.

Non-payment at completion

Client withholds final payment. Fix: deposit + milestone payments so you're never financing the job entirely.

Liability for property damage

Something breaks on site. Fix: liability cap clause + require proof of homeowner's insurance before starting.

Sub causes a problem

Your subcontractor damages the client's property. Fix: subcontractor agreement requiring their own insurance and flow-down liability.

Cancellation after mobilization

Client cancels after you've ordered materials. Fix: cancellation clause with materials reimbursement and mobilization fee.
