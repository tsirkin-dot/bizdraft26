The service-provider risk map

Five risks that drain B2C service businesses. All preventable.

Late cancellations

Client cancels the morning of. You lose the slot revenue. Fix: 24/48-hour cancellation policy with fee in the engagement agreement.

Chargeback disputes

Client disputes a charge with their bank. Fix: written refund policy signed by the client at engagement start.

Liability for injury

Client claims injury during a physical service session. Fix: liability waiver plus professional insurance.

Scope creep

"Can you just also do X?" without additional payment. Fix: clear services-included list and change clause for anything outside it.

Confidentiality breach

Client's financial or personal information disclosed. Fix: confidentiality clause in the engagement agreement for any service involving sensitive data.
