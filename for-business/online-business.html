The online business risk map

Five e-commerce risks. All addressable in writing.

Chargeback exposure

Customers dispute charges with no written policy. Fix: TOS with clear refund policy accepted at checkout.

Supplier non-delivery

Supplier misses an order; you miss your customer commitment. Fix: vendor agreement with delivery SLA and remedy clause.

3PL shipping errors

Fulfillment partner mis-ships. Customer blames you. Fix: 3PL agreement with liability for fulfillment errors.

Digital product piracy

Customer redistributes your digital product. Fix: license agreement with explicit redistribution prohibition and remedy clause.

Contractor IP dispute

Developer claims ownership of the store codebase. Fix: IC agreement with IP assignment for all technical work.
