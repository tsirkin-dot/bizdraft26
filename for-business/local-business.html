The local business risk map

Five risks that surprise brick-and-mortar owners. All preventable.

Lease trap

Signing a landlord's standard lease without understanding the CAM charges or permitted-use restrictions. Fix: lease review before signing.

Employee takes the customer list

Ex-employee solicits your customers. Fix: NDA with non-solicitation clause in every employment agreement.

Vendor non-delivery

Supplier misses an order; you can't serve customers. Fix: vendor agreement with delivery SLA and remedy clause.

Membership dispute

Customer cancels mid-term and demands full refund. Fix: customer engagement agreement with written cancellation and refund policy.

Contractor IP

Marketing contractor owns the brand assets they created. Fix: IC agreement with IP assignment for every external creator.
