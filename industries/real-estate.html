What makes real estate contracts different

Real estate agents work on contingency, share referrals, and hire marketing services. Each relationship needs its own written terms.

BizDraft covers the business relationships around real estate — referral fee agreements, marketing services for listings, co-brokerage arrangements, and agent-team agreements. Full property transaction documents (purchase agreements, listing agreements) are outside this scope and should be handled through your brokerage's forms.

Industry-specific clauses

Referral fee as a percentage of gross commission. Triggering event (closed transaction or signed contract). Exclusivity window. Co-brokerage commission split. Marketing services scope and liability disclaimer. Agent independent contractor terms.

Common deal types

Referral fee agreement between agents, marketing services for listings (photography, staging, paid ads), co-brokerage arrangement, agent-team agreement, and broker-IC relationship.

The real estate risk map

Five real estate business risks. All preventable in the agreement.

Referral fee dispute

The receiving agent closes the deal and doesn't pay the referral fee. Fix: referral fee agreement with triggering event, payment timeline, and dispute resolution clause.

Client poaching

You refer a client; the receiving agent keeps them for future transactions without paying a fee. Fix: exclusivity window and multi-transaction fee clause covering related future business.

Marketing cost recovery

You pay for listing photography and the seller withdraws the listing. Fix: cancellation terms in the marketing services agreement — services rendered are due even if listing withdraws.

Team departure

A buyer's agent leaves the team and takes active client relationships. Fix: client ownership clause defining who retains the relationship with active clients at the time of departure.

Commission split ambiguity

Co-brokerage commission is unclear when the deal closes. Fix: explicit commission split agreement with gross commission as the basis and payment timing at closing.
