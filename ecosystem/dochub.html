How it works

From draft to signed collaborative document: step by step.

The full workflow for team review and collaborative signing.

Step 1

Draft in BizDraft

Go to app.bizdraft.com/editor. Describe the deal. Review the generated agreement and clause explanations. Download the PDF.

Step 2

Upload to DocHub

Go to dochub.com. Create a free account. Upload the agreement PDF. DocHub opens it in the browser-based editor.

Step 3

Share for review

Share the document with reviewers via link or email. Reviewers can add comments, highlight clauses, and ask questions directly on the document. Comment threads stay attached to the relevant clause.

Step 4

Edit and revise

Make revisions based on feedback directly in DocHub's PDF editor. Add text, strike through language, or annotate changes. All revisions are tracked and visible to collaborators.

Step 5

Add signature fields and send

Once the final version is agreed upon, add e-signature fields using DocHub's signature tools. Send to all required signers. They sign from any device — no account required on their end.

Step 6

Download the executed document

The fully signed document is stored in DocHub and available for all parties to download. The complete audit trail — review history, comments, signatures — is preserved.

When BizDraft + DocHub is the right workflow

  • Multiple reviewers before signing. If the agreement needs review by a business partner, an attorney, and the counterparty — DocHub's comment thread model keeps all feedback organized in one document rather than scattered across email chains.
