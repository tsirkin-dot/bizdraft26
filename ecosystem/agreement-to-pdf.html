What this workflow does

From AI draft to managed PDF document.

The three things the PDF workflow accomplishes that downloading alone doesn't.

Edit and mark up the PDF

pdfFiller and DocHub both let you edit PDF text directly, add annotations, highlight specific clauses, add sticky notes, and strike through language — all without converting the file to Word first. The agreement stays in PDF format throughout the review process.

Share for review without email attachments

Share a link to the PDF in pdfFiller or DocHub rather than emailing a file. Reviewers see the document in their browser, leave comments directly on the relevant clauses, and you receive feedback in one organized place — not scattered across an email thread with multiple attached versions.

Store and access the final document

The signed or reviewed document is stored in pdfFiller or DocHub — accessible from any device, sharable with anyone via link, and organized alongside other documents in your account. The cloud storage replaces manual file management on your hard drive.

How it works

Generate to PDF: the complete sequence.

Every step in the PDF agreement workflow.

Step 1

Generate in BizDraft

Go to app.bizdraft.com/editor. Describe the deal. Review the generated agreement. Read the clause explanations for any language you want to understand before editing. When satisfied, download the PDF.

Step 2

Open in pdfFiller or DocHub

Choose the tool based on your need. pdfFiller (pdffiller.com) is stronger for adding fillable fields and reusable templates. DocHub (dochub.com) is stronger for collaborative review and comment threads. Upload the PDF.

Step 3

Mark up the document

Use the annotation tools to highlight clauses you want to discuss, add sticky note comments explaining what needs to change, strike through language that doesn't apply, and add text directly to the PDF where additions are needed.

Step 4

Share for review

Share the document via link or email invitation. Your attorney, business partner, or the other party can view the document and add their own comments and annotations. All feedback is visible to all reviewers in one place.

Step 5

Finalize and save

After review and revisions, save the final version in pdfFiller or DocHub. The document is stored in your account — accessible via web browser from any device, shareable by link, and available for download as needed.

Step 6

Send for signature (optional)

Both pdfFiller and DocHub have e-signature capabilities. Add signature fields and send for signing without leaving the platform. Or download the final PDF and upload to SignNow for the signing workflow. Either path ends with an executed, stored agreement.

pdfFiller vs DocHub: which to use

  • Use pdfFiller if you send the same agreement type to multiple clients and want to create a reusable fillable template with variable fields. pdfFiller's template and form-filling capabilities are its strength.
  • Use DocHub if you need collaborative review with multiple people leaving comments on specific clauses. DocHub's comment thread and collaboration workflow is better suited to multi-reviewer feedback.
  • Use either for straightforward PDF editing, annotation, and single-instance signing. Both handle the basics well and have free tiers that cover standard use.
  • Combine with SignNow if you want to keep signing in the dedicated e-signature platform with a full audit trail. Generate in BizDraft, edit in pdfFiller or DocHub, sign in SignNow.
