How it works

From draft to fillable document: step by step.

The full workflow from deal description to a reusable fillable agreement.

Step 1

Draft in BizDraft

Go to app.bizdraft.com/editor. Describe the deal type and key terms. BizDraft generates the complete agreement. Review the clause explanations. Download the PDF when you're satisfied with the content.

Step 2

Upload to pdfFiller

Go to pdffiller.com. Upload the downloaded PDF. pdfFiller opens it in the browser-based editor — no software to install.

Step 3

Add fillable fields

Use pdfFiller's field tools to add text fields, checkboxes, date fields, and signature fields to the variable sections of the agreement. Place fields where each client needs to fill in their details.

Step 4

Annotate if needed

Add annotations, highlights, or text notes to specific clauses for context. If you're sending this to an attorney for review, you can mark up sections with questions or flag areas for revision directly in pdfFiller.

Step 5

Share with the other party

Share the fillable document via pdfFiller's link or email. The recipient fills in their sections on their device. No account required for the recipient to fill in fields.

Step 6

Download the completed document

Once filled, download the completed PDF. The filled document is ready for signature. For the signature step, use SignNow or pdfFiller's built-in e-signature capability.
